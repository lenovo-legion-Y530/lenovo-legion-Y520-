LENOVO LEGION Y520

ANALYSIS OF THIS GAMING LAPTOP.

THE BOTTOM LINE The Lenovo Legion Y520 is a budget gaming laptop that requires you to make no compromises in build quality or performance. It delivers smooth 1080p gaming at high settings alongside a full feature set.

ANALYSIS

DATA SHEET THE LENOVO LEGION Y520
  • 15.6-inch Full HD screen (1,920 x 1,080 pixels), 250 nits brightness.
  • Intel Core i5-7300H quad-core processor and RAM at 2.3GHz and Turbo Boost up to 4GHz 16GB RAM.
  • NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1060 graphics card.
  • 256 GB PCIe SSD internal memory + 1 TB hard drive (the unit tested).
  • Connections 3 x USB 3.1, 1 x USB C, 1 x Mini Display Port 1.4, 1 x HDMI 2.0, Ethernet Port, 3.5mm Jack.
  • Battery Up to 9.5 hours of use.
